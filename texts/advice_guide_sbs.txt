Step 1: Field Lay Out 

All crop rows should be 65 cm apart from each other, and the distance between the crops in each row should be 75 cm.
If the area is dry, increase the row spacing to 78 cm and crop spacing to 90 cm.
To do this effectively, use a piece of rope for the measurements.
Step 2: The Baisins 
The baisins should be 15 cm long, 8 cm wide and 10 cm deep
 Step 3: Fertiliser and Compost 

The fertiliser should be put in first, under the soil, use a 2 caps of a soda bottle of fertiliser per baisin. 
Then add compost, use a 2 hands of compost per baisin.
