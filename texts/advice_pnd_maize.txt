To protect your maize from pests and diseases, follow these tips: 

- Look out for pests like stem borers, armyworms, and aphids. 
- Watch for diseases such as leaf blight or rust spots. 
- Use natural predators, crop rotation, or safe pesticides to control pests. 
- Inspect your crop regularly for damaged leaves or unusual spots. 
- Avoid over-fertilizing with nitrogen, which can attract pests. 
- Remove or destroy infected plants and crop residues after harvest.
